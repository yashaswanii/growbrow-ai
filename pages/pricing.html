Frequently asked questions

Is there really a free plan? ▼
Yes! Our Starter plan is free forever — no credit card, no expiry. You get 1,000 messages/month and access to core features to test the platform fully.
What is the WhatsApp Business API? ▼
The official API from Meta allows businesses to send messages at scale, build chatbots, and integrate WhatsApp into their systems. GrowBrow.ai is a Meta Business Partner, so we handle your API setup end-to-end.
Are there per-message charges on top? ▼
Meta charges a small per-conversation fee (typically ₹0.25–0.50 for marketing messages). Our platform fee is separate. We show you a transparent cost breakdown before you launch any campaign.
Can I cancel anytime? ▼
Absolutely. No lock-in. Cancel from your dashboard with one click. Annual plan refunds are prorated.
What makes GrowBrow.ai different from AiSensy? ▼
Our Predictive AI Engine sets us apart — it anticipates customer behavior and triggers the right message automatically. We also offer native in-chat payments, AI send-time optimization, and per-message revenue attribution that no other platform has yet.
